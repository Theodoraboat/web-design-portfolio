Theodora is a current junior at the University of Denver studying Strategic Communications and Theatre. She aims combine her interest in performance and journalism to pursue a career in entertainment media. Outside of being a student, Theodora serves as a section editor at the Denver Clarion, the university's paper.

The Clarion introduced Theodora to world of journalism. To her, journalism is a way to connect with communities and earn new perspectives and opportunities by sharing the stories of others.

This website will serve as a portfolio for Theodora's articles. It will feature articles from:

  • Unify
  • News
  • and other sections

Photos taken for articles will also be featured in a gallery.
