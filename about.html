Theodora is a current junior at the University of Denver studying Strategic Communications and Theatre. She aims to combine her interest in performance and journalism to pursue a career in entertainment media. Outside of being a student, Theodora serves as a section editor at the Denver Clarion, the university's paper.

The Clarion introduced Theodora to the world of journalism. To her, journalism is a way to connect with communities and earn new perspectives and opportunities by sharing the stories of others. Through the Clarion, Theodora learned and embraced the importance of local journalism, especially on college campuses. She finds that being in tune with events local to her sets her up for success in areas outside of journalism, such as within her job at DU Admissions and as a performer in the theatre department.

This website will serve as a portfolio for Theodora's articles. It will feature articles from:

  • Unify
  • News
  • and other sections

Photos taken for articles will also be featured in a gallery.
