Welcome to Written by Theodora - a portfolio dedicated to showcasing articles.

Theodora currently writes for the Denver Clarion at the University of Denver and serves as a section editor.

Theodora aims to pursue a career in entertainment media and use this site to display

  • articles from The Clarion
  • photos that accompany each article

She also aims to display skills relevant to her aspirations in media, such as the ability to interview, effective writing, and basic photography, to place readers within the story. These skills are developed and continuously honed weekly either as a writer or as an editor.
